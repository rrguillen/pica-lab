ABOUT US

Welcome you to our website!

This space was created thinking about you, our customer. Our aim for this website is to stay in touch with you. Pica-Lab is a family-owned micro-salsa company based in San Diego, California. At pica-lab we pride ourselves on the quality of our work, and we believe all food can be enhanced with the use of our products. Our salsas are made with fresh, natural ingredients, and free from conservatives. Our unique methods of crafting salsa capture the traditional flavors of smoke and fire.
We appreciate your visit to the Pica-Lab website. We encourage you to contact us about any doubt or suggestion.
Hungrily,
Efren Sandoval
Owner and Cook
